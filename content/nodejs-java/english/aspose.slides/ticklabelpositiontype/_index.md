## TickLabelPositionType class

 Represents the position type of tick-mark labels on the specified axis.
 

## Constants

| Name | Value | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
[High](#High) | 0 | Specifies the axis labels shall be at the high end of the perpendicular axis. |
[Low](#Low) | 1 | Specifies the axis labels shall be at the low end of the perpendicular axis. |
[NextTo](#NextTo) | 2 | Specifies the axis labels shall be next to the axis. |
[None](#None) | 3 | Specifies the axis labels are not drawn. |
