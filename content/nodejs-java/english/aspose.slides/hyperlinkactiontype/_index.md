## HyperlinkActionType class

 Represents a type of hyperlink action.
 

## Constants

| Name | Value | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
[Unknown](#Unknown) | -1 | Unrecognized action type. |
[NoAction](#NoAction) | 0 | No action. |
[Hyperlink](#Hyperlink) | 1 | Usual hyperlink. |
[JumpFirstSlide](#JumpFirstSlide) | 2 | Jump to the first slide of the presentation. |
[JumpPreviousSlide](#JumpPreviousSlide) | 3 | Jump to the previous slide. |
[JumpNextSlide](#JumpNextSlide) | 4 | Jump to the next slide. |
[JumpLastSlide](#JumpLastSlide) | 5 | Jump to the last slide of the presentation. |
[JumpEndShow](#JumpEndShow) | 6 | Jump to the end of slideshow. |
[JumpLastViewedSlide](#JumpLastViewedSlide) | 7 | Jump to the last viewed slide. |
[JumpSpecificSlide](#JumpSpecificSlide) | 8 | Jump to the specific slide, referred by ( IHyperlink#getTargetSlide) property. |
[StartCustomSlideShow](#StartCustomSlideShow) | 9 | Start custom slideshow. |
[OpenFile](#OpenFile) | 10 | Open referenced file. |
[OpenPresentation](#OpenPresentation) | 11 | Open referenced presentation. |
[StartStopMedia](#StartStopMedia) | 12 | Start/stop playing mediafile. |
[StartMacro](#StartMacro) | 13 | Start executing macro script. |
[StartProgram](#StartProgram) | 14 | Start program. |
