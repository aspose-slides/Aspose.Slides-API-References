| Potm | 9 | Microsoft Office Open XML PresentationML 含宏模板。 |
| Otp | 10 | OpenOffice OTP [Presentation](../presentation/) 模板。 |
| Ppt95 | 11 | Microsoft PowerPoint 95 [Presentation](../presentation/)。 |
| Pot | 12 | Microsoft PowerPoint 97-2003 [Presentation](../presentation/) 模板。 |
