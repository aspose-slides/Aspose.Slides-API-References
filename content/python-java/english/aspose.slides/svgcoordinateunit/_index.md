## SvgCoordinateUnit class

 Represents CSS2 coordinate units used to define SVG coordinates.

| Name | Value | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
[Inch](#Inch) | 0 | Inch |
[Centimeter](#Centimeter) | 1 | Centimeter. |
[Millimeter](#Millimeter) | 2 | Millimeter. |
[Point](#Point) | 3 | Point (1/72 of inch), |
[Pica](#Pica) | 4 | Pica (1/6 of inch). |
[Em](#Em) | 5 | Em size of a font of containing element. |
[Ex](#Ex) | 6 | Ex size (size of lowercase letter, usualy "x") of font of containing element. |
[Pixel](#Pixel) | 7 | Pixel size. |
[Percent](#Percent) | 8 | Percent. |
