## ChartSeries.HasUpDownBars property

Determines whether Line- or Stock-chart has a up/down bars. This is the property not only of this series but of all series of parent series group - this is projection of appropriate group property. And so this property is read-only. Use ParentSeriesGroup property for access to parent series group. Use ParentSeriesGroup.UpDownBars.HasUpDownBars read/write property for change value.
