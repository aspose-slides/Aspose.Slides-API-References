## zero_desc property
Gets or sets a value indicating whether the descent (depth below baseline)
            of the base element should be treated as zero.


### Remarks

When `true`, the phantom does not lower the baseline of the surrounding 
            math line. Corresponds to the OMML attribute `m:zeroDesc`.
