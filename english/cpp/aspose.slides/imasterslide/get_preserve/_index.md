## IMasterSlide::get_Preserve() method


Determines whether the corresponding master is deleted when all the slides that follow that master are deleted. Note: [Aspose.Slides](../../) will never remove any unused master by itself, to actually remove unused masters call [IMasterSlideCollection::RemoveUnused](../../imasterslidecollection/removeunused/) Read **bool**.
